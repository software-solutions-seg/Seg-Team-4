Features

Find new people!

Night Out allows you to find new people who's interests align with your own. Using our new propreitary AI machine we are able to match you with someone who will have a very high likelyhood of being your friend. Our test groups have acheived a 95.3% success rate.


Find new places to hangout!

Our app sorts through hundreds of different locations to find those that will best suit your interests. Our state-of-the-art algorithm analyses places that you have previously visited and reviewed to present you with new locations that best suit your interests.


Give your favorite places 5 STARS!

After you visit a new location you can rate it with up to 5 stars. Our algorithm will analyse your reviews to create new suggestions that are tailored to your previous experiences.
